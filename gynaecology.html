Gynecology

GYNECOLOGY

Empowering women to take charge of their health with confidence and care.

Comprehensive Women's Health Care You Can Trust

CARING FOR WOMEN THROUGH EVERY STAGE OF LIFE WITH COMPASSION, EXPERTISE, AND ADVANCED TREATMENT.

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital, we provide expert gynecological care tailored to meet the unique health needs of women at every stage of life — from adolescence to menopause. Our comprehensive services are designed to promote wellness, prevent complications, and manage a wide range of women’s health conditions with compassion and clinical precision.

Led by Dr. K. Jyothi Reddy, a highly experienced gynecologist and laparoscopic surgeon, our team offers personalized care in a safe, supportive environment. Whether you’re visiting for a routine check-up or seeking advanced treatment, we ensure that every woman feels heard, respected, and empowered.

We specialize in diagnosing and treating conditions such as menstrual disorders, PCOS, fibroids, infections, and hormonal imbalances.We also offer preventive screenings like Pap smears and HPV tests, along with minimally invasive surgeries including laparoscopy and hysteroscopy for both diagnosis and treatment.

From reproductive health counseling to menopause management, we are dedicated to supporting your health journey with sensitivity and professionalism. At Rishika, we believe that women’s health is the foundation of strong families and communities — and we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Our Gynecology Services

MENSTRUAL DISORDERS

We provide effective diagnosis and treatment for irregular periods, heavy bleeding, and painful menstruation. Whether it's adolescent menstrual issues or adult cycle concerns, our expert care ensures balance and comfort.

PCOS & HORMONAL IMBALANCE

We specialize in managing Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), thyroid issues, and other hormonal disorders. Our treatments focus on restoring hormonal balance and improving fertility, skin health, and menstrual regularity.

UTERINE FIBROIDS & OVARIAN CYSTS

Accurate diagnosis and treatment options for fibroids and cysts, including medication and laparoscopic surgery. We ensure personalized care based on the severity and patient needs.

INFECTIONS & PELVIC PAIN

From vaginal infections to chronic pelvic pain, we offer prompt care and long-term solutions. Our approach ensures relief and prevention through advanced testing and safe medications.

MENOPAUSE & MIDLIFE CARE

We help women manage menopause symptoms like hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep issues. Our goal is to make this transition smooth with hormone therapy and lifestyle support.

PREVENTIVE SCREENINGS

Regular Pap smears, HPV testing, and breast exams are key to early detection and prevention. We guide you with care and privacy every step of the way.

LAPAROSCOPIC SURGERIES

Minimally invasive procedures for conditions like fibroids, cysts, and endometriosis. Our advanced laparoscopic techniques ensure faster recovery and minimal discomfort.

GENERAL WOMEN'S HEALTH CONSULTATIONS

We provide holistic care for all women’s health concerns — from white discharge to pre-marital counseling and family planning. Every woman receives respectful, confidential, and expert care.

Committed to providing compassionate care for every child, every day.
