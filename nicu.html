Facility Highlights

Advanced Level 3 NICU

Our advanced Level 3 NICU provides critical care with round-the-clock monitoring, offering the highest standard of treatment for critically ill newborns.

Phototherapy for Jaundice Management

Our LED phototherapy (GE) system is equipped for high-intensity phototherapy to effectively manage neonatal jaundice.

Multi-Para Monitors

Our Philips multi-para monitors provide comprehensive data to ensure the well-being of our neonates.

Specialized Warmers

We use WIPRO G.E. Lullaby warmers, specifically designed for premature babies, to ensure optimal comfort and temperature regulation.

Hand Hygiene

We follow strict hand-washing protocols using RO-treated water to maintain the highest standards of cleanliness.
