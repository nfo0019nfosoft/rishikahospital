Operation Theater

NICU

State-of-the-Art Operation Theater at Rishika
Mother and Child Hospital

Precision, Care, and Safety in Every Procedure.

Our operation theater at Rishika Mother and Child Hospital is equipped with the latest surgical technology and designed to provide a sterile, safe environment for pediatric surgeries. With a skilled team of surgeons, anesthetists, and nurses, we ensure that every procedure is performed with the utmost precision and care. Whether it’s a routine surgery or an emergency procedure, we prioritize the health and safety of your child at every step. The theater is also equipped with advanced monitoring systems to ensure continuous oversight throughout the operation. Trust us for expert care during your child’s surgical needs.

Comprehensive Surgical Support: From pre-operative assessments to post-surgical recovery, our dedicated team ensures a seamless, supportive experience for both the child and family throughout the surgical journey.
