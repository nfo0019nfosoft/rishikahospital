Welcome to Our General Pediatric Department

PAEDIATRICS

Helping children discover their inner strength and resilience

OVERVIEW

A Caring and Child-Centered Approach

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital,we understand that every mother and child is unique, with distinct healthcare needs that deserve personalized attention. Our General Pediatric Care department is dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate, and individualized healthcare for children of all ages — from newborns to adolescents. Alongside nurturing their physical health, we also prioritize their emotional well-being, helping them grow into healthy, happy individuals while supporting mothers throughout this journey.

Comprehensive Services for Your Child's Health

Our team of skilled pediatricians and healthcare professionals is committed to providing the highest standard of care. We offer a wide range of services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and the management of common childhood illnesses. Our extended Pediatric OPD hours make it convenient for busy parents to seek medical attention for their children at times that suit their schedules. We also conduct preventive health check-ups and school health check-ups, aiming to identify potential health concerns early and offer timely interventions.

Expert General Pediatric Care at Rishika Mother and Child Hospital

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital, we believe that every child deserves a positive and stress-free healthcare experience. Our hospital is designed to be child-friendly, with a team of caring and supportive nursing staff who ensure that your child feels comfortable and safe during their visit or stay. We aim to create a warm and welcoming environment where parents and caregivers can actively participate in their child’s care and make informed decisions about their health. With our focus on preventive care and education, we empower families to take an active role in their child’s health journey.

Facility Highlights

SPACIOUS AND AIR-CONDITIONED ROOMS

Our Pediatric Ward offers various room options, including Deluxe, Super Deluxe, Twin, and Triple-sharing rooms, all designed to provide comfort and a soothing environment for young patients and their families.

EXTENDED HOURS GENERAL PEDIATRIC OPD

We provide extended hours for our General Pediatric Outpatient Department to better serve the diverse needs of our patients and their families, ensuring convenience and accessibility.

DAY CARE CENTER

For patients requiring short stays or day care surgeries, our Day Care Center is fully equipped to deliver comprehensive care in a comfortable, child-friendly setting..

PATIENT'S EDUCATION PROGRAM

We believe that knowledge is key to promoting health awareness and preventive care. Our educational program empowers families to make informed decisions about their child’s health and well-being.

CARING AND FRIENDLY NURSING STAFF

Our nursing staff is committed to delivering personalized care and support, ensuring that children feel safe, comfortable, and well cared for throughout their stay.

VACCINATION CENTER

We take pride in our unique Vaccination Center, catering to pediatric, adolescent, and adult populations. This center ensures that individuals of all ages have easy access to essential vaccinations.

PREVENTIVE HEALTH CHECKUPS

We offer preventive health checkups to monitor and maintain your child’s well-being. These checkups can catch potential health issues early, allowing for prompt and effective interventions.
