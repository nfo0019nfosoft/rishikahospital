– Patient Information FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our services, treatments, and hospital policies. At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital, we are dedicated to making your visit smooth and providing the best care for both mother and child.

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital, we provide comprehensive care for both mothers and children, including prenatal and postnatal care, general pediatrics, neonatal services, immunizations, management of high-risk pregnancies, treatment of common childhood illnesses, and specialized support for maternal and child health.

Yes, we accept various health insurance plans for cashless and reimbursable treatments for both mother and child care. Please contact us to confirm your specific insurance coverage.

You can call our front desk or use the online appointment system on our website to book a consultation with our specialists in obstetrics, gynecology, and pediatrics.

Yes, we have fully equipped maternity wards and a Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) staffed by experienced neonatologists to care for newborns needing specialized attention.

Our hospital provides 24/7 emergency services with on-site pediatricians and obstetricians to attend to urgent medical needs for both mother and child.
