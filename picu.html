OVERVIEW

Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) at Rishika Mother and Child Hospital

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital,our PICU is a specialized unit where advanced technology, expert care, and compassion come together to provide life-saving treatment. With a dedicated team of pediatric intensivists, we ensure the best possible outcomes for critically ill children. We understand the vital role family involvement plays in the healing process and offer continuous support and communication to help families during this challenging time. Your child’s well-being is our top priority, and we are here to provide expert care every step of the way. Infants to adolescents. We believe in nurturing not just their physical health but also their emotional well-being, ensuring they grow into healthy, happy individuals.

Facility Highlights

State-of-the-Art PICU

Our advanced Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) provides specialized care for critically ill children, ensuring the highest standards of safety and treatment.

Expert Pediatric Intensivists

Our PICU is managed by a team of pediatric intensivists who are available around the clock to provide specialized care.

Ambulance Transport

We provide a well-equipped ambulance service to transport critically ill babies safely and swiftly to our PICU.

Family Support

We prioritize keeping parents informed and supported during critical care, offering regular counseling to address concerns and ensure comfort throughout the process.

Central Monitoring

We use central monitoring with IBP (Invasive Blood Pressure) and EtCo2 (End-Tidal Carbon Dioxide) monitoring to closely track and assess the vital signs of our young patients.

Specialized Procedures

Our PICU provides specialized care, including peritoneal dialysis, CVP monitoring, noninvasive ventilation, and more, to meet each child’s unique needs.

CPAP And HFNC Support

Our PICU offers CPAP and HFNC support to assist with respiratory distress, providing non-invasive solutions to improve oxygenation and ease breathing for critically ill children.

Committed to providing compassionate care for every child, every day.
