OVERVIEW

Advanced Neonatal Care at Rishika Mother and Child Hospital

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital,under the expert guidance of Dr. B. Ramesh and Dr. Swamy Rao, Neonatology is a specialized branch of pediatrics that focuses on the medical care of newborns, especially premature or critically ill infants. Our dedicated neonatologists are skilled in diagnosing and treating newborns with complex health conditions, providing advanced medical care in a compassionate and child-friendly environment.

Comprehensive Care for Newborns

At Rishika Mother and Child Hospital, Jammikunta, we are dedicated to providing holistic care for newborns, ensuring their health and well-being from the very first moments of life. Our state-of-the-art Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is equipped to handle premature births, low birth weight, and critical health conditions with precision and compassion. With a team of skilled specialists and advanced medical technology, we prioritize the safety and comfort of both newborns and their families.
